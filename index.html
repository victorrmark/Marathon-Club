Why become a marathoner?

Running is considered a great form of exercise linked to a number of health benefits.
Moderate running has been linked to a reduced weight, better blood pressure and lower cholesterol.

A few benefits include;

1. You will become a calorie-burning machine.
2. Your diet will begin to improve.
3. You will become more motivated.
4. It's a great way to build your confidence.
5. You can become a happier person.
